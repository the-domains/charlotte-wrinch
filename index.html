charlottewrinch@gmail.com Charlotte Wrinch is a singer-songwriter living on the Sunshine Coast in British Columbia, and is an active member of the Canadian music industry. A FACTOR Demo Award winner, she plays acoustic guitar, piano, and a collection of recorders and penny whistles. She performs regularly at many of the festivals and live music venues on the BC coast.
